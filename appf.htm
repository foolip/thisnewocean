Appendix F
Project Mercury Cost Summaries

Precise costs directly attributable to any research and development program are very difficult to determine, and Project Mercury is no exception in this regard. Overhead and support costs accrued for several projects must be allocated and thus estimates rather than specifics are required. The total summary costs are official agency estimates as of February 28, 1966. Costs within the spacecraft manufacturer's contract are broken down in a second table, and a third details costs incurred by the Department of Defense in support of Project Mercury.
